BBC Market Takeaways — Q2 2024

1

Uluwatu is the top performer for ROI in 2024, with gross yields reaching 15%+ for well-managed luxury villas, driven by rising demand from surfers and digital nomads choosing the Bukit Peninsula over Canggu.

2

Pererenan/Berawa corridor continues to see the highest transaction velocity with strong land appreciation (18-22% YoY). It's the sweet spot between Canggu's price premium and raw growth potential.

3

Ubud is maturing — once seen as a budget alternative, land in the Ubud core is now IDR 5-8M/sqm. The wellness tourism boom is driving strong occupancy, particularly for eco-villa concepts.

4

Leasehold remains dominant for foreign investors due to simpler structuring. Average lease terms are 25-30 years with extension options, and the market is increasingly accepting longer 50-year deals.

5

Watch the regulatory landscape — Indonesia continues to discuss new pathways for foreign property ownership (Golden Visa tier 2, long-stay visa real estate). Engage a trusted legal advisor before transacting.
